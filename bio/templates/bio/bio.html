About James

James is a first class session artist. Not only has he styled hair for the likes of Italian Vogue and other high end publications he has worked his magic on celebrity artists such as Camilla Cabello. James not only can be found at Rose&Wild Hair Salon, but he can be seen heading the hair on the X-Factor Tour. Working with noteworthy designers at fashion week and collaborating with industry experts creating fashion and editorial campaigns. He has a flair for precision cutting which includes all types of male grooming and fashion forward styling but specialises in beautiful and effortless finishes. James’s clients really can’t get enough of his professionalism and undeniable sense of taste and style.
